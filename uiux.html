FAQ

Design Questions

What's the difference between UI and UX design?
UX (User Experience) focuses on the overall feel and journey — how easy and enjoyable it is to use a product. UI (User Interface) is the visual layer — colours, typography, buttons, and visual elements. Both work together to create great products.
Do I get source design files?
Yes! You receive all source files (Figma, Adobe XD, etc.), a complete design system, style guide, and developer-ready assets. Full ownership is transferred to you upon project completion.
How many design revisions are included?
We include unlimited revisions within the project scope. Our collaborative process ensures you're involved at every stage, so major changes are rare by the time we reach the final design phase.
