FAQ

Printing Questions

What file formats do you accept?
We accept all major file formats including PDF (preferred), AI, PSD, EPS, CDR, JPG, and PNG. For best results, please submit print-ready PDF files with 300 DPI resolution, CMYK color mode, and 3mm bleed. If you don't have design files, our team can create designs for you at no extra cost.
What is the turnaround time?
Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days for most products. Business cards and flyers can be delivered in 24-48 hours with our express service. Large format printing and specialty finishes may take 5-7 days. Rush orders are available for urgent requirements — just let us know your deadline.
What is the minimum order quantity?
Minimum order quantities vary by product. Business cards start at 100 pieces, brochures at 50 pieces, and flyers at 100 pieces. For banners and signage, there's no minimum — we can print even a single piece. Bulk orders receive significant discounts, so ordering more saves you money per unit.
Do you offer design services?
Yes! We offer free basic design services with all print orders. Our designers can create professional designs based on your requirements, logo, and brand guidelines. For complex design projects, custom design packages are available. We also offer unlimited revisions until you're completely satisfied.
Do you provide samples before bulk printing?
Absolutely! We provide digital proofs for approval before printing. For large orders or premium finishes, we can create physical samples (additional charges may apply). This ensures you see exactly what you'll receive before committing to the full order. We don't proceed without your approval.
Do you deliver across India?
Yes! We deliver to all major cities across India. Local delivery (within city) is free for orders above ₹2,000. For outstation delivery, shipping charges are calculated based on weight and location. Express delivery options are available for urgent orders. We also ship internationally on request.
