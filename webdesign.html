FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
Typically 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. Simple sites can be done in 2-3 weeks, while complex e-commerce or custom applications may take 3-6 months.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! Every website we build is fully responsive and tested across all devices and screen sizes — desktop, tablet, and mobile.
Can I update the website myself?
Yes! We integrate easy-to-use CMS (Content Management Systems) so you can update content, add blog posts, and manage products without any technical knowledge.
Do you provide hosting services?
Yes! We offer reliable, fast hosting solutions. We can also work with your existing hosting provider if you prefer.
