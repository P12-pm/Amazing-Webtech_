How long does it take to develop an app?
Simple apps take 2-4 months. Medium complexity apps take 4-8 months. Enterprise apps with complex features can take 8-12+ months. We provide detailed timelines after our discovery phase.
Do you develop for both iOS and Android?
Yes! We build native apps for both platforms and cross-platform solutions using React Native or Flutter, which can reduce cost and development time significantly.
Will you help publish to app stores?
Absolutely! We handle the complete App Store and Google Play submission process, including all required assets, metadata, and compliance requirements.
