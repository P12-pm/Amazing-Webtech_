FAQ

Video Questions

How long does it take to produce an animated video?
Production time depends on complexity, length, and style. A 60-second 2D explainer video typically takes 3-4 weeks. 3D animation requires more time — usually 4-8 weeks for a minute of content. Rush projects can be accommodated with adjusted timelines. We'll provide a detailed schedule during our initial consultation.
What's the difference between 2D and 3D animation costs?
3D animation typically costs 2-3x more than 2D due to the additional complexity involved — modeling, texturing, lighting, and rendering. However, 3D offers photorealistic visuals that 2D cannot achieve. 2D is excellent for explainers and character-driven content, while 3D shines for product visualization and architectural walkthroughs.
Do you provide voiceover and music?
Yes! All our packages include professional voiceover and royalty-free background music. We have access to voice artists in multiple languages and accents. You can choose from our recommendations or provide specific direction. Custom music composition is also available for premium projects.
How many revisions are included?
Our standard packages include 2-3 rounds of revisions at each stage — storyboard, design, and final animation. We get your approval before moving to the next stage, which minimizes major changes later. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds can be accommodated at an hourly rate.
What file formats will I receive?
We deliver your final video in multiple formats optimized for different platforms: MP4 (H.264) for general use, MOV for high quality, and specific formats for YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and TV broadcast. We can also provide 4K resolution, vertical versions for social media, and GIF exports if needed.
Can you create animations in different languages?
Absolutely! We work with clients globally and can produce videos in any language. We have voiceover artists for Hindi, English, Spanish, French, Arabic, and many more languages. We also offer subtitle integration and can create multiple language versions of the same video at discounted rates.
