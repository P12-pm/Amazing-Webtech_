FAQ

Branding Questions

What's included in a complete branding package?
A complete branding package typically includes logo design (primary and secondary versions), color palette, typography selection, brand guidelines document, business card design, letterhead, social media templates, and various file formats for all assets. We customize packages based on your specific business needs.
How long does the branding process take?
The timeline depends on the scope of your project. A basic logo design can take 1-2 weeks, while a complete brand identity package typically takes 4-6 weeks. Enterprise-level branding with strategy can take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
How many logo concepts will I receive?
Depending on your package, you'll receive 3-5 initial logo concepts. After your feedback, we refine the chosen direction with multiple revisions until you're completely satisfied. Our goal is to create a logo that perfectly represents your brand.
Do you offer brand refresh or rebranding services?
Yes! We offer both brand refresh (modernizing existing elements while maintaining recognition) and complete rebranding services (creating an entirely new identity). We'll assess your current brand and recommend the best approach based on your goals and market position.
What file formats will I receive?
You'll receive all brand assets in multiple formats including AI, EPS, SVG (vector formats for print), PNG, JPG (web formats), and PDF. We also provide different color versions (full color, black, white, grayscale) optimized for various applications.
