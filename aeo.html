FAQ

AEO Questions

What exactly is AEO and why does it matter?
AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) focuses on optimising your content to appear as direct answers in AI assistants, voice search, and featured snippets. With AI-powered search growing rapidly, AEO is becoming as important as traditional SEO.
How is AEO different from SEO?
Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in list-based search results. AEO focuses on becoming the definitive answer that AI tools and voice assistants cite directly — often meaning your brand gets mentioned even without a website visit.
Can AEO and SEO work together?
Absolutely! They complement each other perfectly. Strong SEO provides the foundation, while AEO ensures you capture the growing share of AI-powered searches. We often implement both strategies simultaneously.
