FAQ

Hosting Questions

What is web hosting and why do I need it?
Web hosting is a service that stores your website files on a server, making them accessible on the internet. Every website needs hosting to be visible online. Think of it as renting space on the internet where your website lives. Without hosting, no one can access your website.
What's the difference between shared and cloud hosting?
Shared hosting places multiple websites on a single server, sharing resources — it's affordable but has limited resources. Cloud hosting distributes your website across multiple servers, providing better reliability, scalability, and performance. Cloud hosting is ideal for growing businesses that need guaranteed resources.
Do you offer free SSL certificates?
Yes! All our hosting plans include free SSL certificates via Let's Encrypt. SSL encrypts data between your website and visitors, displays the padlock icon, and is essential for SEO and user trust. Installation is automatic and renewals are handled for you.
Can you migrate my existing website?
Absolutely! We offer free website migration for all new customers. Our expert team will transfer your files, databases, emails, and configurations with zero downtime. Simply provide your current hosting details, and we'll handle the entire process within 24-48 hours.
What is your uptime guarantee?
We guarantee 99.9% uptime, meaning your website will be accessible virtually all the time. Our infrastructure includes redundant systems, automatic failover, and 24/7 monitoring. If we fail to meet this guarantee, you're eligible for service credits as outlined in our SLA.
How do backups work?
We perform automatic daily backups of your entire account including files, databases, and emails. Backups are stored for 30 days on separate servers. You can restore your website with one click from cPanel, or contact our support team for assistance with restoration.
