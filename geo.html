FAQ

GEO Questions

What exactly is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?
GEO is the practice of optimizing your online presence to be recognized, cited, and recommended by AI-powered search engines and language models like ChatGPT, Google's AI Overview, Perplexity, and Claude. Unlike traditional SEO which focuses on ranking in search results, GEO ensures your brand becomes the trusted source that AI systems reference when answering user queries.
How is GEO different from AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)?
While both focus on AI, AEO primarily targets featured snippets, voice search, and direct answers in traditional search. GEO goes further by optimizing for generative AI systems that create comprehensive responses. GEO focuses on entity building, knowledge graph presence, and ensuring AI language models understand and trust your brand as an authoritative source across all AI platforms.
Do I still need SEO if I invest in GEO?
Yes! SEO and GEO work together synergistically. Strong SEO provides the foundation of quality content and authority signals that AI systems use to determine trustworthiness. GEO then ensures this content is structured and positioned to be cited by AI. We recommend a combined SEO + GEO strategy for maximum visibility across both traditional and AI-powered search.
How do you measure GEO success?
We track multiple GEO-specific metrics including: AI citation frequency (how often your brand is mentioned in AI responses), knowledge panel presence, entity recognition tests across AI platforms, brand mention sentiment in AI outputs, referral traffic from AI platforms, and comparative analysis against competitors in AI responses.
How long does it take to see GEO results?
GEO is a long-term strategy. Initial improvements in AI visibility can be seen within 2-3 months, but significant results typically appear in 4-6 months. Building strong entity presence and authority takes time as AI systems need to crawl, process, and trust your content. However, early investment provides lasting competitive advantage as AI adoption accelerates.
Can you guarantee my brand will appear in ChatGPT responses?
No ethical agency can guarantee specific AI responses, as AI systems are constantly evolving and responses vary based on context. However, we significantly increase your probability of being cited by building the trust signals, entity presence, and content structure that AI systems prefer. Our strategies are based on research into how LLMs source and prioritize information.
