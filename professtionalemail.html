FAQ

Email Questions

Do I need my own domain for professional email?
Yes, professional email requires a domain name (like yourbusiness.com). If you already have a domain, we can set up email on it. If not, we can help you register one — many of our plans include a free domain for the first year. Your domain is your business identity online.
Can I access my email on multiple devices?
Absolutely! Your professional email syncs across all your devices — desktop, laptop, tablet, and smartphone. Use webmail from any browser, or set it up in your favorite email client like Outlook, Apple Mail, or Gmail. All your emails, contacts, and calendars stay in sync everywhere.
How secure is your email service?
We use enterprise-grade security including SSL/TLS encryption for all emails in transit, anti-spam and anti-virus filtering, phishing protection, and two-factor authentication. We also support SPF, DKIM, and DMARC for email authentication. Your business communications are fully protected.
Can you migrate my existing emails?
Yes! We offer free email migration from any provider including Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, GoDaddy, or any other email service. Our team will transfer all your emails, contacts, calendars, and folder structures with zero data loss and minimal downtime. The process is completely hands-free for you.
How many email addresses can I create?
Our plans are priced per user, so you can add as many email accounts as your business needs. Need 5 team members? Pay for 5 users. Growing to 50? Just add more users anytime. You can also create unlimited email aliases and forwarding addresses at no extra cost.
What's included in the storage limit?
Storage includes all your emails (sent and received), attachments, contacts, and calendar events. Our Basic plan offers 5GB per user, Business offers 30GB, and Enterprise includes unlimited storage. If you need more storage on lower plans, we can customize your package.
