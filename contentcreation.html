FAQ

Content Questions

What types of content do you create?
We create a wide range of content including social media videos (Reels, TikToks, Stories), YouTube videos, product photography, lifestyle photography, blog articles, website copy, email content, infographics, ad creatives, corporate videos, testimonial videos, podcast production, and more. Our team handles everything from concept to final delivery.
How long does it take to create content?
Turnaround time depends on the content type and complexity. Social media graphics typically take 2-3 days, short-form videos 3-5 days, blog articles 3-5 days, and professional video productions 1-2 weeks. For ongoing content packages, we work on a monthly content calendar with scheduled delivery dates.
Do you provide content strategy or just creation?
We provide both! Our team develops comprehensive content strategies including audience research, content pillars, posting schedules, and performance tracking. We believe great content starts with great strategy, so all our packages include strategic planning to ensure your content achieves your business goals.
Can you match our brand voice and style?
Absolutely! We start every project with a brand immersion session to understand your brand voice, visual style, target audience, and competitors. We create content that feels authentically yours while bringing fresh creative ideas. If you have brand guidelines, we follow them precisely; if not, we can help develop them.
How many revisions are included?
All our packages include unlimited revisions to ensure you're 100% satisfied with the final content. We typically deliver content with 2-3 rounds of revisions built into the timeline. Our goal is to create content you love, so we'll keep refining until you're completely happy with the results.
Do you handle posting and scheduling?
Yes, we offer full social media management services including content scheduling and posting. However, if you prefer to handle posting yourself, we'll deliver all content with captions, hashtags, and optimal posting times so you can easily upload to your accounts. We're flexible to work however suits you best.
