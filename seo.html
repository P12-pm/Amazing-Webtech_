FAQ

SEO Questions

How long does it take to see SEO results?
SEO is a long-term strategy. You may see initial improvements within 2-3 months, but significant results typically appear in 4-6 months. For competitive industries, it can take 6-12 months to achieve top rankings. The timeline depends on your website's current state, competition level, and the aggressiveness of your SEO campaign.
How much does SEO cost?
SEO pricing varies based on your goals, competition, and scope of work. Our packages start from ₹15,000/month for local businesses and go up to ₹1,00,000+/month for enterprise-level campaigns. We offer customized plans based on your specific needs and provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.
Do you guarantee #1 rankings on Google?
No ethical SEO agency can guarantee specific rankings, as Google's algorithm considers hundreds of factors and constantly changes. Be wary of agencies that make such promises. What we do guarantee is implementing proven best practices, transparent reporting, and continuous optimization to improve your rankings and organic traffic over time.
What's the difference between SEO and PPC?
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on earning organic (free) traffic through improved rankings, while PPC (Pay-Per-Click) involves paying for ads that appear at the top of search results. SEO provides long-term sustainable results, while PPC offers immediate visibility but stops when you stop paying. We recommend combining both for maximum impact.
Will you need access to my website?
Yes, to implement technical SEO fixes and on-page optimizations, we'll need access to your website's backend (CMS), Google Search Console, and Google Analytics. We take security seriously and sign NDAs to protect your information. All access credentials are stored securely and only used for optimization purposes.
How do you report on SEO progress?
We provide comprehensive monthly reports covering keyword rankings, organic traffic, backlinks acquired, technical health score, and conversions. You'll also get access to a real-time dashboard to track progress anytime. We schedule monthly calls to discuss results, answer questions, and plan next steps.
