FAQ

Common Questions

How much should I budget for digital marketing?
Budget depends on your goals, industry, and competition. For small businesses, we recommend starting with ₹25,000-₹50,000/month for basic campaigns. Medium businesses typically invest ₹75,000-₹1,50,000/month, while larger campaigns may require ₹2,00,000+ monthly. We'll help you determine the right budget during our consultation.
How long does it take to see results?
PPC campaigns can show results within days of launching. Social media marketing typically takes 2-3 months to build momentum. Email marketing results vary based on list quality. For comprehensive digital marketing strategies, expect meaningful results within 3-6 months, with continuous improvement over time.
Which platforms should my business be on?
This depends on your target audience and business type. B2C brands often succeed on Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube. B2B companies typically focus on LinkedIn and Google Ads. E-commerce businesses benefit from Google Shopping and Instagram. We analyze your audience and recommend the most effective platforms for your specific goals.
Do you provide detailed reporting?
Absolutely! Transparency is core to our approach. You'll receive detailed monthly reports covering all KPIs including impressions, clicks, conversions, cost per acquisition, ROI, and more. We also provide real-time dashboard access and schedule regular strategy calls to discuss performance and optimizations.
Can you work with my existing marketing team?
Yes! We frequently collaborate with in-house teams. Whether you need us to handle everything, manage specific channels, or provide strategic guidance while your team executes, we adapt to your needs. We can also provide training to help upskill your internal team.
What makes your agency different?
We combine data-driven strategies with creative excellence. Unlike agencies that use one-size-fits-all approaches, we create customized strategies for each client. Our team includes certified Google, Facebook, and HubSpot experts. We focus on ROI, not vanity metrics, and maintain complete transparency in our reporting and billing.
